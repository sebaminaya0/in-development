This is the story of how the Iron Man died

The discovery of a new inhuman, who can see the future, has prompted civil war among the super human community. Much like its predecessor (Civil War 2006), Civil War 2016 will focus on a moral and ethical decision of acting on future premonitions shown to the heroes by the inhuman Ulysses. This will divide the community in half pitting Carol Danvers (Captain Marvel) against Tony Stark (Iron Man). Follow us as we cover Marvel’s Civil War II 2016.
